## My library!

My project is a digial library where users can interactively get readings based on a theme. 

How to use:
- Run it by going to https://jia-o.github.io/library/ 
- Once you click the screen, your mouse will lock and become a direction controller while your keys are used to walk around
- When you walk up to a table and click the jar, you will get a reading that corresponds to the labeled theme of the jar
- You can directly read it by clicking the popup or put it back
- Google doc API is used and kept secret through Render

What I like about it:
- The placement of the jars on the tables and tables on the floor
- The wallpaper that makes a continuous image
- The matching of jar colour, reading theme, and table label
- The fact that it allows me to narcissistically use *myself* as a database
